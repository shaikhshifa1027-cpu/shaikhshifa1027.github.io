Pizza (Italy)

Pizza is one of the most popular foods in the world and originates from Italy. It consists of a flat round base of dough topped with sauce, cheese, and various ingredients.

Main Ingredients

  • Wheat flour dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Vegetables or meat toppings (such as mushrooms, olives, peppers)

Pizza is baked in an oven until the cheese melts and the crust becomes crispy.

Sushi (Japan)

Sushi is a famous Japanese dish made with vinegared rice and various ingredients such as seafood and vegetables.

Main Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Rice vinegar
  • Nori (seaweed sheets)
  • Fresh fish or seafood
  • Vegetables like cucumber or avocado

Sushi is often served with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

Tacos (Mexico)

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made with folded tortillas filled with a variety of ingredients.

Main Ingredients

  • Corn or wheat tortillas
  • Grilled meat or beans
  • Chopped onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Salsa sauce

Tacos are popular street foods and can be prepared with many different fillings.

Fried Rice (China)

Fried Rice is a popular Chinese dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, eggs, and sauces.

Main Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables (carrots, peas, onions)
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Cooking oil

Sometimes chicken, shrimp, or tofu is added for extra flavor.

Butter Chicken (India)

Butter Chicken is a famous Indian dish known for its creamy tomato-based gravy and rich flavor.

Main Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Tomato puree
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Garlic and ginger paste
  • Spices such as garam masala, turmeric, and chili powder

It is usually served with naan bread or rice.

Croissant (France)

Croissant is a popular French pastry known for its flaky and buttery texture.

Main Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Yeast

The dough is layered with butter and baked until golden brown. Croissants are commonly eaten for breakfast with coffee or tea.

Pad Thai (Thailand)

Pad Thai is a famous Thai noodle dish known for its sweet, sour, and savory taste.

Main Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Eggs
  • Tofu or shrimp
  • Bean sprouts
  • Tamarind paste
  • Fish sauce
  • Peanuts
  • Lime

Pad Thai is stir-fried and usually served with crushed peanuts and lime.
